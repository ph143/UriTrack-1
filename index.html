Why It Matters?

Diseases often start at a zero state and progress silently before symptoms arise. Many chronic disorders go unnoticed until significant, irreversible damage has occurred. Late detection limits treatment to managing the disease rather than curing it.

Our Solution

We provide a smart, automated urine analysis system that seamlessly profiles urine and alerts users to potential health red flags. Our system offers:

  • Regular urine monitoring without requiring any extra effort.
  • Early detection of abnormalities with actionable insights.
  • Personalized lifestyle recommendations and medical prompts when needed.

How It Works

Regular Monitoring

Our system ensures consistent monitoring, eliminating the need for manual effort.

Early Detection

Identifies abnormalities at their earliest stages and provides actionable insights.

Personalized Recommendations

Offers tailored lifestyle advice and prompts medical attention when necessary.
